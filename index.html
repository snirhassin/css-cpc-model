FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Your $500 becomes your CPC balance. When shoppers click on your products through our network and are redirected to your Amazon listing, the click cost is deducted from this balance. You'll have a real-time dashboard showing every click, the remaining balance, and performance metrics. Your dedicated Account Manager will help optimise your listings from day one.
We use Amazon's Attribution programme to generate tagged links for your products. When a shopper clicks through from Royal Deals and makes a purchase on Amazon, the sale is tracked and attributed to our traffic. This gives you verified, Amazon-confirmed conversion data — not estimates. You can also see this data in your own Seller Central dashboard.
Once we have enough attribution data to demonstrate consistent conversion performance (typically after your initial $500 balance is spent), your Account Manager will review the results with you. If the data shows strong ROI, we'll propose a RevShare rate based on your product category and sales volume. The transition is entirely optional — you can continue with CPC if you prefer.
Creator Connections is our network of product reviewers, comparison writers, and content creators who produce authentic editorial content featuring your products. This drives organic, high-converting traffic through trusted recommendations. When you connect your Seller Dashboard, you can see exactly how creator-driven traffic performs alongside our comparison site traffic.
Yes, you can top up your CPC balance at any time in any amount. If you decide to stop, any unused balance can be refunded. There are no lock-in contracts. Many of our partners top up several times before transitioning to RevShare, while others move to RevShare after their first $500.
Revenue Share is calculated as a percentage of the confirmed sale value attributed to our traffic through Amazon Attribution. The exact percentage varies by product category and is agreed upon with your Account Manager. All sales are verified through Amazon's own reporting — you'll never pay commission on a sale we didn't drive. Typical RevShare rates range from 3-8% depending on category and volume.
Our attribution and RevShare model is optimised for Amazon sellers (via Amazon Attribution and Seller Central integration). However, if you sell through your own website, we can still work with you on a CPC basis with standard conversion tracking. Contact our team to discuss your specific setup.
